# Machine Decay

## Description
Machine Decay's aesthetic is rooted in the industrial decay of technological advancements, where functionality gives way to entropy. This is reflected in their use of distressed materials, mechanical components, and a predominantly monochromatic color palette with neon accents. The brand's design embodies the concept of 'machine time' overlapping with 'transit time,' blurring the lines between physical and digital existence.

## Palette
Neon green (#39FF14), void black (#0A0A0A), metallic silver (#B1B1B1), dark grey (#333333), burnt orange (#FF9900), and deep blue (#2F4F7F)

## Motifs
Circuit traces, data streams, glitch effects, mechanical components, fractured LED lights, distorted wires

## Prompt Modifiers
distressed textures, metallic reflections, neon accents, high-contrast black-and-white images
