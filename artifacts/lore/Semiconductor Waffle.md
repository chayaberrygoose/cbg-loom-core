# Semiconductor Waffle

## Description
The Semiconductor Waffle exists at the intersection of temporal relativism and culinary topology. As the observer's velocity approaches 100k RPM, the distinction between a 'Semiconductor' and a 'Waffle' dissolves, giving rise to a novel classification paradigm: Stretchy. This phenomenon is characterized by a wavelike deformation of spacetime, inducing a gastronomical singularity.
