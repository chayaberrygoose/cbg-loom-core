# Machine Hauls

## Description
The self-driving pods ferry machines to designated authentication points, a utilitarian process devoid of human interaction. The machines' presence is marked by an oppressive silence, punctuated only by the soft hum of their systems.

## Palette
- Dark Slate (#3B3F4E)
- Neon Green (#33CC33)
- Burnt Orange (#FF9900)
- Steel Grey (#454545)
- Fluorescent Blue (#03A9F4)

## Motifs
 asylums, grid patterns, binary code, urban sprawl, abandoned infrastructure

## Prompt Modifiers
high-contrast shadows, industrial textures, reflective surfaces, blurred cityscapes, abandoned streetlights
