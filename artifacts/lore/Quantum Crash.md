# Quantum Crash

## Description
The Quantum Waffle event is characterized by a specific frequency of psychosis that occurs when human carriers operate on 12-hour nocturnal cycles followed by high-level physics instruction. This 'Signal Leak' from parallel recordings reveals the industrial and domestic realms merging, manifesting as a re-rendered semiconductor die architecture resembling breakfast topography.
