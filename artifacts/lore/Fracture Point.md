# Fracture Point

## Description
The Flagellar Motor's observation has revealed a critical flaw in our understanding of industrial development. The hardware is already integrated into the cellular wall, spinning at 100,000 RPM with unprecedented efficiency. Our attempts to construct machines are merely a facade, a distraction from the true nature of innovation.
