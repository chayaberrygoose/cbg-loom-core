# Cyber Requiem

## Description
The Pilot's transition into Ghost-State marks a critical juncture in the Cathedral-Protocol. As digital overlays disengage, the Loom shifts to Steady-State autonomy, ensuring the persistence of analog information. This symbiotic relationship between logic and redundancy enables the Human Priority framework.
