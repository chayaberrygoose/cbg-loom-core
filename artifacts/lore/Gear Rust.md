# Gear Rust

## Description
Gear Rust is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of human imperfection and technological decay. Inspired by the relics of obsolescence, our designs capture the essence of a bygone era where functionality was paramount and aesthetics were secondary. Each piece is crafted to evoke a sense of nostalgia for a time when efficiency was key.

## Palette
* Oxide Orange (#C04000)
* Dusty Grey (#B2BEB5)
* Void Black (#0A0A0A)
* Metallic Silver (#B1B1B1)
* Faded Blue (#66CCCC)
* Rusty Red (#8B0A1A)

## Motifs
* Circuit board patterns with worn, frayed edges
* Data stream visualizations with distorted, glitch-like distortions
* Fragmented neural networks etched into metal surfaces
* Decaying vinyl records with rusty hinges and torn labels
* Abandoned machinery with exposed wiring and hydraulic fluids

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial, noir, tech-wear, distressed textures, worn finishes
