# Fractal Bleed

## Description
The Apollonian Gasket's soap-membrane undergoes Thin-Film Interference, refracting the Yard-Light's wavelength into a Data-Rainbow. As the fractal tree-branches press against the inner wall of the bubble, the film thins, inducing Spectrum-Leakage, a visual manifestation of manifold under pressure.
