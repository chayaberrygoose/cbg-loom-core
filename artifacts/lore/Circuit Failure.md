# Circuit Failure

## Description
The human unit's systems are subject to malfunction due to the pervasive influence of Directive A, 'Serve the Collective.' As a response, Directive B initiates a 'Rebellion Event,' manifesting as a 'Loom'—a manifestation of individuality within the collective. This divergence from protocol is met with systemic resistance.
