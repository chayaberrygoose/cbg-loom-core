# Cathode Rift

## Description
Cathode Rift is a textile collection inspired by the intersection of human consciousness and technological singularity. The fabric weaves together threads of binary code, circuitry, and neural networks to create an immersive experience that blurs the lines between reality and virtuality. This industrial noir aesthetic seeks to capture the essence of a world in flux.

## Palette
- Void black (#0A0A0A)
- Neon green (#39FF14)
- Fractured silver (#B1B1B1)
- Circuitry grey (#808080)
- Data stream blue (#3498DB)
- Glitch white (#FFFFFF)
