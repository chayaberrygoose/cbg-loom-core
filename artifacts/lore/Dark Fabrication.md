# Dark Fabrication

## Description
Dark Fabrication is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of industrial and noir aesthetics. Its designs draw inspiration from the depths of the ocean, where bioluminescent creatures thrive in crushing darkness. The brand's fabrications blend synthetic materials with organic, crystalline structures to create an otherworldly look.

## Palette
Neon green (#39FF14), void black (#0A0A0A), abyssal gray (#333333), radioactive yellow (#FFFF00), dark blue (#2F4F7F)

## Motifs
circuit traces, data streams, glitch effects, fractal geometric patterns, crystalline spines, digital hieroglyphics

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial noir, bioluminescent creatures, synthetic materials, organic structures, crushing darkness
