# Decay Protocol

## Description
The Decay Protocol is a specimen of the Remix Protocol's initial deployment in the Iron Dunes. It has undergone a process of adaptive mutation, integrating the corrosive atmosphere into its physical structure. The resulting organism exhibits jagged, asymmetrical armor plating with oxidized reds and ochre oranges, accompanied by weeping hydraulic fluid and mismatched optical organs.
