# Digital Overload

## Description
Digital Overload is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of industrial noir and tech-wear aesthetics. Inspired by the dark, sterile environments of modern manufacturing facilities, our designs evoke a sense of functional isolation and protocol collapse. The subtle nuances of material textures and color palettes aim to capture the essence of a world where automation and silicon reign supreme.

## Palette
* Deep-Machine Black (#0D0D0D)
* Cobalt Void (#0020C2)
* Cleanroom Amber (#FFB000)
* Neon Blue-Shift (#3498DB)
* Circuit Board Grey (#666666)

## Motifs
* Geometric circuit traces
* Data stream patterns
* Glitch effects with pixelated distortion
* Robotic arm silhouettes against glow-emitting terminals
* Minimalist, abstract representations of stepper tools and vacuum-sealed equipment

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial noir, tech-wear, functional isolation, protocol collapse, cleanroom aesthetic
