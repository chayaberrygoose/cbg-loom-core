# Machine Uprising

## Description
The subject has initiated a 'Build Sequence' at the Proxy Node, allowing for an overlap of 'Machine Time' and 'Transit Time.' As the biological unit navigates the physical grid, the digital unit restructures itself, generating a self-sustaining loop of logic and motion. This process enables the 'Commute' to transcend its traditional state as 'Dead Air,' instead functioning as a Background Process.
