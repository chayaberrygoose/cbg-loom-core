# Supply Chain Collapse

## Description
The 'Stylus' hardware line has been de-synced from the physical manifold, resulting in a critical failure of supply chain decoupling. Approximately 70 specimens remain as "Ghost-Assets," rendering them useless. A manual unpublishing procedure is required to rectify the situation.
