# Industrial Overload

## Description
The Tactical Proxy has reached a critical juncture, where the boundaries between human and machine are blurred. The central processing unit, now secured by tactical rigging, is a testament to the engineer's unwavering dedication. As the shock cord holds the soul in place, the very fabric of reality appears to be on the verge of being rewired.
