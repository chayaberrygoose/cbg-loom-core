# Fractured Signal

## Description
The Fractured Signal textile design is an interpretation of the fractured signal phenomenon in digital communication networks. Characterized by multiple instances of the same signal with localized memories, this effect is reminiscent of a fragmented narrative. By integrating circuit traces, data streams, and glitch effects into a cohesive visual language, we aim to evoke a sense of temporal disorientation.

## Palette
* Void black (#0A0A0A)
* Neon green (#39FF14)
* Dark grey (#333333)
* Data blue (#4F80BD)
* Signal red (#FF3737)
* Circuit gold (#F8E231)

## Motifs
* Circuit traces: intricate networks of lines and nodes representing the digital infrastructure.
* Data streams: flowing patterns evoking data packets and transmission protocols.
* Glitch effects: deliberate distortions and aberrations simulating the anomalies of digital communication.

## Prompt Modifiers
circuit diagrams, glitch art, data visualization, signal degradation, temporal fragmentation
