# Circuit Reckoning

## Description
Circuit Reckoning is a textile brand that embodies the aesthetic of industrial noir, where functionality and innovation converge with an affinity for the darker aspects of technology. Our designs are inspired by the intricate networks of circuitry and the eerie beauty of malfunctioning machinery.

## Palette
- Void Black (#0A0A0A)
- Neon Green (#39FF14)
- Frostbite White (#FFFFFF)
- Cyber Grey (#333333)
- Circuit Blue (#032B44)

## Motifs
- Circuit Traces
- Data Streams
- Glitch Effects
- Fractured Grids
- Synthetized Memories

## Prompt Modifiers
 industrial noir, cyberpunk textures, glitch art, circuit board patterns, neon-lit urban landscapes
