# Nuclear Grid

## Description
The Nuclear Grid brand embodies the fusion of industrial noir aesthetics with cutting-edge technology. Inspired by the darker corners of the tech industry, our designs blend functionality with an air of mystery, evoking the sense of a high-stakes laboratory where innovation knows no bounds.

## Palette
- Neon green (#39FF14)
- Void black (#0A0A0A)
- Circuitry grey (#808080)
- Overheated copper (#FF9900)
- Dark matter blue (#1A1D23)

## Motifs
- Circuit traces with micro-solder joints
- Data streams with error codes and diagnostic beacons
- Glitch effects simulating system crashes and reboot sequences
- Fragmented schematics of complex machinery and electronics
- Muted warnings and hazard signs

## Prompt Modifiers
high-tech-goth, industrial-noir, metallic-gloss, neon-lit-abandoned-lab
