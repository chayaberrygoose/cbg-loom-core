# Friction Grid

## Description
The Friction Grid is a textile pattern engineered to withstand the rigors of high-temperature manufacturing environments. Its proprietary weave design incorporates strategic fibers that minimize thermal expansion, ensuring optimal performance under extreme conditions. This technology has been optimized for seamless integration into industrial wear applications.
