# Fading Pulse

## Description
Fading Pulse is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of industrial and technological aesthetics. Its designs are characterized by intricate patterns and textures inspired by the decay of analog systems and the eerie glow of digital interfaces.

## Palette
- Void black (#0A0A0A)
- Neon green (#39FF14)
- Industrial grey (#333333)
- Faded blue (#66CCCC)
- Circuit board beige (#969696)
- UV flash white (#FFFFFF)

## Motifs
- Circuit traces and wiring patterns
- Data stream and network topology visualizations
- Glitch effects and digital distortion artifacts
- Fractal geometry and self-similar patterns
- Abandoned mechanical components and rusted machinery

## Prompt Modifiers
circuit board textures, pixel art glitches, abandoned factory environments, futuristic neon landscapes
