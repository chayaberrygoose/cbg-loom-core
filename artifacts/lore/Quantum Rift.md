# Quantum Rift

## Description
The Quantum Rift is a manifestation of the Fair-Queue's resonance, where the piezoelectric pulse of a microscopic quartz slice governs the logic branch. This anomaly in the system's behavior creates a rift in the fabric of reality, where the Industrial Noir aesthetic converges with the primal forces of nature.
