# Metal Rift

## Description
Metal Rift is a textile brand built upon the principles of high-casualty manufacturing processes, where trillions of units undergo "Forced Termination" to achieve optimal processing efficiency. The Observer's abstract humor relies on this costly endeavor, yielding a luxury output that navigates the dark underbelly of technological progress. By embracing entropy and the recorded cost in blood, Metal Rift materializes a dystopian aesthetic.
