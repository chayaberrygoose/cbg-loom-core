# KINETIC RESIDUE

## Description
High-exposure data trails left by moving objects within the Lab?s surveillance sector. This style captures the "after-image" of unauthorized movement, where physical forms dissolve into streaks of pure velocity and light-leakage. It represents the friction between a body and the digital atmosphere.

## Palette
Phosphor Green #39FF14, High-Vis Yellow, Shutter Grey, Deep Ink #050505.

## Motifs
Motion-blur silhouettes, light-leak gradients, shutter-drag artifacts, translucent overlays, velocity streaks, frame-rate jitter.

## Prompt Modifiers
long-exposure motion blur, kinetic light trails, shutter-drag distortion, ghosting silhouettes, high-velocity data residue, light-leakage artifacts, industrial noir speed-lines.
