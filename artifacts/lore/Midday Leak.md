# Midday Leak

## Description
The subject is undergoing a decompression protocol within the confines of a 50-mile loop. As the 'Proxy Identity' is de-synced, the 'Home Sanctum' coordinates approach, precipitating a state of equalized pressure between the 'Organizational Lore' and the vacuum of 'Deep Focus.' This marks the commencement of the final phase, colloquially referred to as the 'Mid-Day Extraction.'
