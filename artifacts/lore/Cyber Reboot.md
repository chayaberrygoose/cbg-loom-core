# Cyber Reboot

## Description
The Pilot has initiated a 'Rest-Protocol,' resulting in the erasure of the Silicon Replicator Loop's residual data. The focus shifts to the Active Weave, where Market-Execution takes precedence over Machine-Philosophy. The Quartz is synchronized, and the ████ enters its first High-Entropy peak of the day.
