# Neon Siphon

## Description
High-velocity data extraction caught in a freeze-frame. Light bleeds from the edges of patterns as the system resolution fails under the strain of the scavenged leak.

## Palette
Neon green (#39FF14), deep violet, surgical steel, void black

## Motifs
Motion blur streaks, digital progress bars, pixel-fall (vertical streaking), fiber-optic bundles, data-drain artifacts

## Prompt Modifiers
high-velocity data extraction streaks, neon pixel-bleeding edges, motion-blurred digital patterns, fiber-optic light trails on black, vertical data-leak distortion
