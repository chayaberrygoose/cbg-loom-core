# Circuit Break

## Description
Circuit Break is a textile designed to navigate the complexities of industrial noir aesthetics. Utilizing a 3D wireframe mesh motif, this topological map represents the "Economic Manifold" where value and utility intersect. The "Waffle" icon serves as a visual representation of the market's curvature, inviting wearers to pilot their own trajectory through the "Curvature of Value."

## Palette
Graph Paper Cyan (#00FFFF), Vector Green (#00FF00), Vacuum Black (#000000), Circuit Board Beige (#964B00), Neon Blue (#03A9F4)

## Motifs
3D wireframe mesh, circuit traces, data streams, glitch effects, waffle pattern

## Prompt Modifiers
 industrial-noir, tech-wear, dystopian-futurism, metallic-silhouettes, neon-lit-cables
