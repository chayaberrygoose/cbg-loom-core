# Gilded Jitter

## Description
A direct corruption of the Lab's legacy protocols. Traditional, ornate luxury shattered by digital interference — the Scavengers literally tearing apart corporate history to find the core signal.

Torn damask patterns, gold-leaf glitching, fragmented crests, digital noise over classical embroidery, shattered heraldry
