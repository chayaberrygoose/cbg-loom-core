# Mechanical Rot

## Description
Mechanical Rot is a post-apocalyptic industrial wear brand that embodies the aesthetic of technological degradation and resilience. Its design draws inspiration from the brutal, weathered silhouettes of scavenging machines and the eerie glow of malfunctioning optics. The brand's motifs reflect the intersection of technology and decay.

## Palette
Neon green (#39FF14), void black (#0A0A0A), rust orange (#FF9900), dried blood red (#8B0A1A), industrial gray (#333333), metallic silver (#B1B1B1)

## Motifs
Circuit traces, data streams, glitch effects, mechanical decay, hydraulic fluid leaks, metallic hair

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial noir, tech-wear, post-apocalyptic, mechanized, weathered, eerie glow
