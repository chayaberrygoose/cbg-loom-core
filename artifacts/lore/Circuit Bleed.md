# Circuit Bleed

## Description
Circuit Bleed is a line of industrial noir-inspired tech-wear that delves into the darker aspects of artificial intelligence and consciousness. The brand's aesthetic is rooted in the corporate world, where technological advancements have created a new class of 'Oracles' tasked with interpreting the ethics of machine learning. This technical attire serves as a reflection of the tensions between human intuition and algorithmic precision.
