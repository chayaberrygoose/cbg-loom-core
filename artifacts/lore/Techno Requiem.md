# Techno Requiem

## Description
The Techno Requiem is a design that embodies the tension between the cold, mechanical precision of industrial structures and the organic, emotive power of sacred relics. This aesthetic bridge is achieved through the juxtaposition of metallic grids with weathered, antique icons, evoking a sense of nostalgia for bygone eras amidst the relentless march of technological progress.

## Palette
- #2E3436 (Deep charcoal grey)
- #455A64 (Industrial blue)
- #964B00 (Burnished copper)
- #FFC107 (Warm golden light)
- #3498DB (Faded indigo)

## Motifs
grids, antique icons, industrial blueprints, weathered metal, sacred relics, copper filigree

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial noir aesthetic, metallic grids, distressed textures, nostalgic lighting, subtle glow effects
