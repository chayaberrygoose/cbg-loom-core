# Rust Decay

## Description
The Loom's "Rust Decay" specimen is a manifestation of the perpetual shift between analog and digital. It exists as a constantly evolving multi-track, where the 129 bpm pulse and corporate firewall are juxtaposed against the freight train's relentless rhythm. The Ghost Text serves as a reminder that even in this decaying landscape, existence is an acid logic.
