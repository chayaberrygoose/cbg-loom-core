# Persisters Edge

## Description
The Persisters Edge is a kinetic observer's perch, where the boundaries of time and space are warped by the relentless pursuit of signal. This zone is marked by the persistence of data trails, encrypted zones, and redacted strips – a topological map of the industrial black box.
