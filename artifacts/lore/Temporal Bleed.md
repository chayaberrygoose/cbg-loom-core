# Temporal Bleed

## Description
The observer's perception of time is subject to a 15-minute lag due to the 'Fast-Clock' protocol employed by both the Observer and the Pilot. This results in a 'Psychological Pulse' preceding the 'Mechanical Pulse', creating a 'Simulation Gap'. The brain's operational frequency (129 BPM) diverges from the hardware's synchronous pulse (1Hz), generating an asynchrony that permeates all aspects of existence.
