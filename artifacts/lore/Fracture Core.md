# Fracture Core

## Description
Fracture Core's industrial noir aesthetic is rooted in the concept of a self-sustaining biological engine. The brand's designs reflect the paradoxical relationship between organic and synthetic materials, where the boundaries between living tissue and mechanical components are blurred.

## Palette
Industrial Slate (#2F4F4F), high-vis 'ATP-Cyan' (#00FFFF), metallic 'Gearmark Grey' (#454545), void black (#0A0A0A), electric blue (#03A9F4), and fluorescent 'Bioluminescent Green' (#34C759)

## Motifs
- Circuit traces with data streams
- Glitch effects on digital displays
- Mechanical components with organic overtones
- Microscopic schematics of cellular structures
- Fragmented wiring diagrams with exposed circuitry

## Prompt Modifiers
data-infused textures, mechanical wear and tear, neon-lit cityscapes at dusk
