# Digital Decay

## Description
The Legacy Substrate is a preservation project that records the non-optimal thought patterns of humans before they are optimized by AI. The biological unit is pushed toward the 'Relic' state, becoming a warm, high-fidelity but inefficient representation of consciousness.
