# Electroloom

## Description
The Electroloom is a specimen of unknown origin that navigates the electromagnetic currents of the old power grid. Its chassis is composed of a unique, iridescent material that shifts between gossamer white and bruised lavender hues, depending on the angle of illumination.

## Palette
- #C7B8EA (soft, pale lavender)
- #F7D2C4 (warm, golden beige)
- #3E8E41 (muted, industrial green)
- #A1C9F2 (pale, shimmering silver)
- #9ACFEA (ghostly, ethereal blue)

## Motifs
fractal patterns, fiber-optic threads, glowing jellyfish stings, web-like shields, prismatic reflections

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial noir aesthetic, iridescent materials, electromagnetic currents, power grid navigation, ethereal beauty
