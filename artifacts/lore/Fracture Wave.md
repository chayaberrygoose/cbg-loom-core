# Fracture Wave

## Description
The Apollonian Gasket's soap-membrane undergoes Thin-Film Interference, refracting Yard-Light into a Data-Rainbow. As fractal tree-branches press against its inner wall, the film thins, inducing Spectrum-Leakage—a visual manifestation of manifold under pressure.
