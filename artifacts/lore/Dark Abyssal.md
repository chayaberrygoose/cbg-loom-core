# Dark Abyssal

## Description
The Dark Abyssal is a bioengineered specimen exhibiting characteristics of deep-sea gigantism, with a primary carapace composed of light-absorbing abyssal black. Its synthetic skeletal structure features jagged, crystalline protrusions along its spine, which display pulsing, toxic-cyan bioluminescence.
