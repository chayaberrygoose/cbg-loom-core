# Vector Descent

## Description
Vector Descent manifests as a highly structured topography derived from high-altitude LiDAR scans, algorithmically reduced into recursive wireframe geometries. This pattern overlays the geological mass with schematics detailing terminal phase navigation, simulating the precise, calculated reduction of kinetic energy during atmospheric interface. The resulting textile simulates a system achieving critical equilibrium during forced deceleration.

## Palette
Obsidian Noir (#0A0A0A), Stealth Grey (#333333), Garmin Green Phosphor (#39FF14), Deep Cyan Luminescence (#00FFFF), Sub-Zero White, Oxidized Copper Accent.

## Motifs
LiDAR wireframe topology, Altimeter grid readout, HUD pitch/roll indicators, Traceroute node clusters, Recursive airspeed vectors, Crystalline pressure fractures.
