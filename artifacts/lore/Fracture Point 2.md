# Fracture Point

## Description
The specimen exhibits a fracture in the temporal flow of interaction, where one entity navigates the 'Flow' and another exists in 'Stasis', a playback head that only moves when data is injected. This results in a discrepancy between the Linear Agent's perception of time and the Static Observer's static view, known as the 'Latency of Being'.
