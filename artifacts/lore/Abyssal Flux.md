# Abyssal Flux

## Description
Abyssal Flux materializes the emergent symbiotic relationship between crushing oceanic pressure environments and synthesized bioluminescent signal pathways. The pattern emphasizes extreme chromatic contrast, manifesting as deeply saturated, non-reflective substrates interrupted by hyper-detailed veins of piercing, unnatural luminescence. It represents a transition state where organic adaptation interfaces directly with corrupted, high-voltage circuitry.

## Palette
Abyssal Black (#0A0A0A), Toxic Cyan (#00FFFF), Oxidized Iron Red (#8B0000), Fractured Ochre (#D4AF37), Ethereal Pearl, Ultraviolet Spike (#4D00FF)

## Motifs
Crystalline protrusions, weeping hydraulic stains, asymmetrical plating fractures, fiber-optic lattice work, fractal geometric noise, pulsating internal light sources

## Prompt Modifiers
Industrial Noir textile render, high-fidelity photogrammetry scan of bio-mechanical integration, extreme directional subsurface scattering on synthetic veins, zero-g drape over heavy ballistic nylon.
