# Spark Containment

## Description
The Spark Containment protocol was initiated in response to an anomalous biological event. A singular entity, designated 'Subject Alpha,' exhibited unexplained metabolic enhancements, resulting in accelerated cellular regeneration and heightened cognitive function. The containment facility's primary objective is to understand and harness this phenomenon.
