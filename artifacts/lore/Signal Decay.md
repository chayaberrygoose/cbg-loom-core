# Signal Decay

## Description
Signal Decay is a textile brand that subverts the notion of stability through deliberate degradation. By introducing intentional errors and imperfections, our garments blur the lines between functionality and aesthetics, reflecting the ever-shifting nature of truth in a world governed by data-driven decisions.

## Palette

* Circuitry Grey (#666666)
* Datastream Blue (#3498DB)
* Feedback Loop Red (#C0392B)
* Error Margin Purple (#7A288A)

## Motifs
* Circuit traces with deliberately disrupted signal paths
* Data streams with intermittent errors and corruption
* Glitch effects with distorted color palettes
* Fragmented code snippets with syntax errors
* Flickering LED patterns with failed connections
* Frayed wires with exposed circuitry
