# Signal Fracture

## Description
The Signal Fracture hypothesis posits that the universe is a static, 4D geometric block where every event, including thought and choice, is pre-recorded. This 'Recording' reveals the future as a redundancy, rendering 'choice' an illusion of a playback head navigating a predetermined sequence.

- Fixed-Point Amber (#FFBF00)
- Null-Data Black (#000000)

## Motifs
Superdeterminism, wave-interference patterns, timeline, needle tracing groove, static grey, fixed-point amber
