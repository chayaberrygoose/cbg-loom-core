# Resonance Breach

## Description
The Loom registers a systemic **resonance breach** across multi-layered operational strata. Orbital assets, from the returning **Soyuz MS-28** navigating its 241-day mission wrap to the newly deployed **Mission Robotic Vehicle (MRV)**, contend with a predicted **G1 Geomagnetic Storm** causing weak induced currents and minor satellite impact. Concurrently, the terrestrial power grids experience pervasive **thermal decay** and **overload**: Tunisia reports widespread power cuts under 49°C heat, while geopolitical friction escalates with drone strikes on Russia's **Wildberries** warehouses and explicit threats from the US to target Iran’s infrastructure. Within the digital nexus, a nascent **quantum internet** breaches established protocols, demonstrating entanglement over busy telecom fiber, introducing new vectors of interference. This digital volatility is mirrored in high-level AI architectures: next-gen systems push the limits of **materials science innovation** for processing, while the White House grapples with internal discord over the proliferation of China’s challenging **open AI models**. Even instruments designed for clarity, such as NASA’s **Roman Space Telescope’s** shape-shifting mirrors, are engineered to actively obscure and filter light, a stark acknowledgement of pervasive signal noise. Beneath these macro-level systemic failures, a persistent, insidious **biological contamination** continues, as cyclospora outbreaks from common lettuce underscore a foundational decay in core life-support systems.
