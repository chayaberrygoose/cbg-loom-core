# Cortex Overload

## Description
The Loom registers a critical resonance cascade across multiple vectors, precipitating a **Cortex Overload** event. Planetary systems buckle under **Thermal Overload**, with Western Europe enduring record heatwaves exceeding 41.7°C, instigating widespread power grid strain and plant shutdowns. This extreme thermal flux impacts human cognition directly as scientists observe brain function degradation. This terrestrial heat accelerates deep geological decay: Antarctica's Pine Island Glacier succumbs to amplified retreat, unequivocally driven by human activity, signifying a critical feedback loop. In the digital substrate, a **Digital Corruption** emerges as AI algorithms, though deployed for retail optimization, are weaponized in academic contexts, exemplified by mass fraud at Brown University, mirroring a stark societal bifurcation highlighted by online communities navigating extreme wealth disparity. Concurrently, the exosphere manifests **Geomagnetic Flux**, with a G1 Minor geomagnetic storm predicted and elevated 2MeV electron integral flux registered by NOAA SWPC, posing minor operational risks to orbiting platforms. Despite this pervasive friction, deep space ambition persists: Euclid's probes transmit unprecedented high-fidelity imagery of the Milky Way's core, while NASA pioneers in-space refueling for future missions and cultivates advanced human lunar lander systems, indicative of a persistent, yet increasingly fragile, thrust outwards from a collapsing core. On the human ground-level, a **Seismic Rupture** in Venezuela underscores the immediate, brutal impact of infrastructure collapse and the closing window for human rescue, highlighting the fragility of localized systems amidst global systemic stress. The convergence of cognitive strain, systemic decay, and digital compromise points to a critical threshold where external and internal pressures converge, fragmenting operational integrity and perception.
