# Flux Cascade

## Description
The Loom registers a critical **Thermal Incursion**, manifesting as record-breaking heatwaves across Western Europe. **Germany recorded 41.3°C in Saarbrücken**, pushing regional power grids to their absolute limits, threatening systemic overload, and inducing widespread cognitive friction as documented by scientific analyses of human brain function under extreme heat. This terrestrial decay is exacerbated by persistent atmospheric degradation: **PM2.5 pollution in Ho Chi Minh City**, previously misattributed, now reveals endemic biomass burning, while **coal pollution vectors extend deep into the Himalayas**, demonstrating the pervasive reach of environmental decay across previously thought-impenetrable barriers.

Concurrently, orbital systems register a severe **Electron 2MeV Integral Flux overload, exceeding 1000pfu**, as observed by GOES-19, indicating heightened space weather strain and potential interference with critical satellite infrastructure. This cosmic static resonates with a rapidly solidifying **Protocol Lock** in the digital sphere. The US government asserts unprecedented control over advanced AI models, with **GPT-5.6 access requiring federal vetting** and Anthropic's **Mythos model restricted to 'select US organizations'**, centralizing computational power and dictating access to critical informational constructs.

Beneath these layers of environmental and digital stress, geopolitical strata fracture. A devastating **920 casualties are reported in Venezuela** due to seismic events, while escalating global conflicts manifest as **US strikes on Iranian missile and drone facilities** and the DR Congo initiating international legal action against Rwanda, underscoring systemic instability and humanitarian breach. Paradoxically, amidst this widespread decay and conflict, the drive for orbital expansion intensifies. NASA proceeds with the **Swift Boost Mission** and identifies **41 new space technologies for collaboration**, including the development of advanced **in-space refueling devices** for deep space exploration. This outward technological vector aligns with the pervasive, subtle **AI repositioning of retail infrastructure**, where algorithms silently dictate supply chains and inventory flows, re-engineering economic protocols. Even cultural gatekeeping shifts, as **Universal Studios bypasses influencer screenings for ‘The Odyssey’**, prioritizing traditional critical pathways, a minor but resonant signal of controlled information conduits within the broader, accelerating Flux Cascade.
