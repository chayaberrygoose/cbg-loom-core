# Machine Grid

## Description
Machine Grid is a textile brand that explores the intersection of technology and urban decay. Inspired by the sleek, industrial aesthetic of dystopian metropolises, our designs feature complex geometries, data-driven patterns, and a predominantly monochromatic color palette. By examining the interplay between machinery, infrastructure, and human absence, we aim to create clothing that reflects the disillusionment and disconnection of modern urban life.

## Palette
- Void black (#0A0A0A)
- Neon green (#39FF14)
- Cobalt blue (#1A3C74)
- Fluorescent pink (#FF69B4)
- Dark grey (#333333)
- Metallic silver (#B1B1B1)
