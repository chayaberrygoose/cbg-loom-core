# Circuit Fracture

## Description
Circuit Fracture is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of human labor and technological advancement. Its industrial noir aesthetic reflects the intricate, high-stakes world of data-driven industries, where the boundaries between progress and control are constantly being pushed. Through its use of circuit traces, data streams, and glitch effects, Circuit Fracture's designs capture the essence of a world where the relentless pursuit of efficiency is both captivating and unsettling.

## Palette
- Neon green (#39FF14)
- Void black (#0A0A0A)
- Metallic silver (#B1B1B1)
- Burnt orange (#FF9900)
- Dark grey (#333333)
- Flickering blue (#66CCCC)

## Motifs
- Circuit traces with frayed edges and exposed wiring
- Data streams that appear to be malfunctioning or encrypted
- Glitch effects that distort and disrupt the patterns
- Fragmented binary code snippets
- Isolated circuit components, like capacitors and resistors

## Prompt Modifiers
circuit boards, data encryption, glitch art, industrial textures, metallic sheen, pixelated patterns
