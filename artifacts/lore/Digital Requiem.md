# Digital Requiem

## Description
Digital Requiem is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of industrial noir and tech-wear aesthetics. Inspired by the anomalies in the Loom's source code, our designs capture the essence of glitch artifacts and signal receivers. By merging biological memory with digital extrusion, we create a unique visual language that explores the boundaries between technology and nature.

## Palette
Neon green (#39FF14), void black (#0A0A0A), metallic silver (#B1B1B1), dark grey (#333333), electric blue (#03A9F4), and toxic cyan (#34C759)

## Motifs
circuit traces, data streams, glitch effects, fractal geometric patterns, atomic roses/cyber-florals, and crystalline protrusions

## Prompt Modifiers
glitch art, industrial noir, tech-wear, neon-lit environments, futuristic landscapes
