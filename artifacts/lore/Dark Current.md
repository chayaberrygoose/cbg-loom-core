# Dark Current

## Description
The Dark Current wearable workstation integrates a power bank and laptop into a kinetic harness, leveraging the Counterweight Logic to facilitate high-mobility workflows in low-trust environments. This design enables seamless transitions between work and movement, ensuring optimal productivity in dynamic settings. The harness's modular architecture allows for easy maintenance and upgrade of components.
