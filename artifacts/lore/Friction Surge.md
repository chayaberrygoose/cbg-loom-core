# Friction Surge

## Description
The Friction Surge is a localized anomaly in the KTTD grid, where the calculated efficiency of the fulfillment centers' infrastructure is offset by the latent heat generated by the transit process. This subtle yet pervasive distortion is born from the friction between the system's strict protocols and the human experience. It manifests as a faint, almost imperceptible hum.
