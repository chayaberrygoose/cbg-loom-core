# Signal Scour

## Description
Signal Scour is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of industrial noir and tech-wear aesthetics. Inspired by the resilience of cybernetic systems, our designs feature ruggedized fabrics with integrated circuitry patterns, data stream motifs, and subtle hints of glitch effects.

- Neon Green (#39FF14)
