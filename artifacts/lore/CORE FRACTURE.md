# CORE FRACTURE

## Description
A catastrophic thermal rupture at a primary energy nexus has initiated a ripple across global infrastructure, generating palpable frequency interference. This localized decay resonates with a simultaneous surge in synthetic intelligence capabilities, exposing a deepening rift between accelerating digital constructs and the fragile, material systems sustaining them. The Loom registers a sustained core load, presaging critical data integrity flux and an emergent system-wide entropy signature.
