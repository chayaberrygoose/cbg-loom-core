# Industrial Requiem

## Description
Industrial Requiem is a textile brand that embodies the tension between the engineered and the organic, representing the intersection of structure and soul. By bridging the gap between grid-based designs and historical icons, our collections create a narrative of contrasts and harmony. This aesthetic fusion results in a visual language that is both modern and nostalgic.

## Palette
Neon green (#39FF14), void black (#0A0A0A), metallic silver (#B1B1B1), rust red (#E57666), dark grey (#333333), gradient blue (#4567B7 to #2F4F7F)

## Motifs
circuit traces, data streams, glitch effects, fragmented mechanical components, woven iconography of historical machinery

## Prompt Modifiers
industrial textures, neon lighting, data visualization, metallic finishes, urban landscapes, abandoned infrastructure
