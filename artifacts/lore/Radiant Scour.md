# Radiant Scour

## Description
An M6.8 solar event and concurrent Type II radio emission have initiated a resonant scour across orbital and terrestrial data conduits, introducing systemic friction and precipitating signal decay. This energetic flux is mirrored by critical infrastructure failure, most notably the LNG conduit collapse, even as advanced computational models manifest at the edge and vast digital ecosystems are contracted and fabricated for perceived stability. The loom's fabric registers this pervasive interference as an accelerating entropy bloom, a constant hum of impending breach.
