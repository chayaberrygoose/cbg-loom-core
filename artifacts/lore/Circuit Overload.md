# Circuit Overload

## Description
Circuit Overload is a textile brand that embodies the intersection of industrial noir and tech-wear aesthetics. Inspired by the fragmented memory banks of an ancient motherboard, our designs evoke the eerie beauty of "glitch space" – areas where code and logic collide. Each piece is crafted to reflect the tension between digital precision and organic imperfection.
